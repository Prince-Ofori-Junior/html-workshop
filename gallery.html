My Life Journey As A Web Developer.

At first i thought it was an easy task to develop a website until i started practicing it myself day and night to overcome my fears in developing a standard projects. I started watching you-tube videos from a content creator called Bro code, because freecodecamp was new to me and do not understand anything on the site. So i overcome every fear and started freecodecamp with ease practice. After everything i started building a some standard website and also able to apply css to my website.

But what has motivated me most was freecodecamp certification projects thats is acquire at every end of a session. And with persistent hard work i am able to acquire a certificate from freecodecamp.
